Broccoli is a nutritious green that can be eaten raw, steamed, blanched, boiled and nearly every way imaginable. All parts of the Broccoli can be eaten although most only eat the tops. Broccoli has a long growing time and needs an early start. Where winters are long Broccoli needs to be planted indoors and then transplanted into a vegetable garden if you do not have a greenhouse.
